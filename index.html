What is Stock Market?

The stock market is a platform where buyers and sellers meet to trade stocks. A stock represents a share in the ownership of a company.

Types of Stocks

  • Common Stock: Gives shareholders voting rights but no guaranteed dividend.
  • Preferred Stock: Provides no voting rights but usually guarantees a fixed dividend.

How Stock Market Works?

The stock market works like an auction where investors buy and sell shares of publicly listed companies.

Fundamental Analysis

Fundamental analysis involves analyzing a company's financials like revenue, profit, and growth potential to determine its stock value.
